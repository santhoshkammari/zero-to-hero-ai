# Ch12 Nice to Know - Research Summary

## Topics to Cover
1. **Tokenization Quirks** - Glitch tokens, SolidGoldMagikarp, BPE anomalies
2. **Scaling Hypothesis Debates** - Chinchilla scaling laws vs overtrained models (Llama 3 trained on 15T tokens for 8B model)
3. **LLM Psychology** - Sycophancy, sandbagging, mode collapse in behavior
4. **Mechanistic Interpretability** - Induction heads, superposition, polysemanticity, Anthropic's work
5. **Compute Governance** - AI chips export controls, frontier model regulation, US/EU policies
6. **Energy/Environmental Costs** - GPT-3: 1.3 GWh, GPT-4: 5-10 GWh, inference costs exceed training
7. **Copyright & Training Data** - NYT v. OpenAI, fair use debate, memorization vs learning
8. **The Bitter Lesson** - Rich Sutton 2019, compute > hand-engineering

- GPT-4 training: 5-10 GWh estimated, 2500-5000 tonnes CO2
