# Ch15 Nice to Know - Research Summary

## Topics to Cover
1. Neural Style Transfer - Gatys 2015, content/style loss, gram matrix, fast style transfer
2. Deepfakes & Detection - autoencoders, GANs, face swapping, detection methods
3. Adversarial Examples for Generative Models - perturbations that fool generators
4. Score Matching & Langevin Dynamics - Song & Ermon 2019, bridge to diffusion
5. Poisson Flow Generative Models - PFGM 2022, electrostatics analogy
6. Neural ODEs for Generation - Chen 2018, continuous normalizing flows
7. Discrete Diffusion for Text - D3PM, MDLM 2024, masked diffusion
8. Autoregressive Image Models - PixelCNN, VQVAE, VQGAN progression
9. Creative AI Tools Ecosystem - Midjourney, Runway, Suno, ElevenLabs, Stable Diffusion

## Key Insights
- Score matching is the mathematical bridge between EBMs and diffusion models
- PFGM uses electrostatics analogy - data points as electric field sources
- Neural ODEs replace discrete layers with continuous dynamics, enabling CNFs
- Discrete diffusion (MDLM) is now competitive with autoregressive LMs
- PixelCNN -> VQVAE -> VQGAN is a clear evolutionary chain
- Creative AI tools form a multimodal pipeline: image -> video -> audio -> voice
