## Merge Decisions

- **s01 + s02 → s01**: Preprocessing and classical representations are one continuous pipeline. Reader sees the full journey from raw text to TF-IDF vectors in one section.
- **s06 + s09 → s05**: Generation and translation are both seq2seq tasks. Translation drove the invention of attention, so the narrative flows naturally from decoding strategies to MT.
- **s08 → s07 (Nice to Know)**: HuggingFace tooling, evaluation metrics, data augmentation, and explainability are practical tools that don't need deep treatment — brief entries with code and "when you'd use this."
