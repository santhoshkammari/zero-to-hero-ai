# Chapter 17 — Learning Theory & Advanced ML

## Status: ✅ v3 COMPLETE — Variable-depth, concept-driven, conversational tone

## Author Intent
1. **RL intuition is critical** — reader must understand MDP → Q-learning → policy gradient → PPO pipeline, because this is the foundation for RLHF
2. **Causal inference awareness** — correlation ≠ causation, and now the reader knows the actual tools (do-calculus, backdoor criterion)
3. **Generalization theory basics** — PAC learning, VC dimension. Not for proofs, but for intuition about "how much data do I need?"
4. **Advanced toolkit landscape** — sparse methods, factorization, kernels. Know they exist, know when to reach for them
5. **Variable depth** — RL goes DEEP, learning theory MODERATE, sparse/kernels BRIEF, nice-to-know AWARENESS
