# Chapter 15 — Generative Models

## Status: ✅ v3 COMPLETE — Variable-depth, concept-driven, conversational tone

## Author Intent
1. **The generative landscape** — Reader understands the evolution: AEs → VAEs → GANs → Diffusion, and WHY each transition happened
2. **Diffusion is king** — The deep section. Reader should be able to explain forward/reverse process, noise prediction, CFG, latent diffusion, and the U-Net/DiT architecture
3. **GANs in context** — Historical importance, why they were dominant, why diffusion won (mode collapse, training instability)
4. **Evaluation matters** — FID, CLIP score, human eval. Can't ship generative models without knowing how to measure quality
5. **Reality-first** — Focus on what's used in production (Stable Diffusion, DALL-E, ControlNet) not theoretical curiosities
6. **Modern awareness** — Consistency models, flow matching, DiT, video generation. What's current.
