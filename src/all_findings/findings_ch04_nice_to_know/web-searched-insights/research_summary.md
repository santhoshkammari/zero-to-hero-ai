# Web Search Research Summary

## Sources Consulted
- Curse of dimensionality: Aggarwal et al. (2001), Bellman (1957), Géron (2019)
- Manifold hypothesis: LeCun, Bengio, Hinton (2015) Nature review
- NFL: Wolpert & Macready (1997)
- PAC: Valiant (1984), Murphy (2012) Ch.1
- Conformal prediction: Vovk et al. (1999), MAPIE docs, arxiv 2107.07511
- Interview gotchas: Various ML engineering blog posts and interview guides

## Key Validated Insights
1. Distance concentration in high dimensions is mathematically proven
2. Manifold hypothesis is empirically well-supported but not formally proven
3. NFL theorem is frequently misquoted — it's about averaging over ALL distributions
4. PAC bounds are conceptually important but practically loose
5. Conformal prediction is gaining traction in production (MAPIE library)
6. Double descent is well-documented but not yet fully theoretically explained
