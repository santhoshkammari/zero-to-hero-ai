# Chapter 13 — ML Systems and Production

## Status: ✅ v3 COMPLETE — Variable-depth, concept-driven, conversational tone

## Author Intent
1. **THE production chapter** — reader finishes ready to design, deploy, monitor, and operate ML systems
2. **Reality-first** — not theory, not textbook. What actually happens when you ship models.
3. **System design interview-ready** — covers the full arc interviewers expect
4. **Variable depth** — system design and MLOps get deep treatment; federated learning gets a Nice to Know mention
5. **Production gotchas** — the things that bite: training-serving skew, silent model degradation, cost surprises
