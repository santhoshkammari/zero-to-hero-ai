# Ch16 Nice to Know - Research Summary

## Topics to Cover
1. Kolmogorov-Arnold Networks (KANs) - learnable univariate functions on edges vs nodes, spline-based, 2024
2. Liquid Neural Networks - MIT, ODE-based continuous-time, adaptive time constants, C. elegans inspired
3. Hypernetworks - networks that generate weights for other networks, David Ha 2016
4. Neural Cellular Automata - learned local update rules, self-repair, morphogenesis, Mordvintsev 2020
5. Boltzmann Machines Revival - energy-based models, RBMs, modern EBMs
6. Reservoir Computing - echo state networks, random fixed weights, only train readout layer
7. Spiking Neural Networks - event-driven, temporal coding, neuromorphic chips (Intel Loihi)
8. Differentiable Rendering - NeRF, 3D Gaussian Splatting, inverse graphics
9. Neural Implicit Representations - DeepSDF, occupancy networks, continuous 3D
10. AI for Science - AlphaFold 3, GenCast weather, GNoME materials discovery
